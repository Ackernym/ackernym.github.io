ABOUT ME

contact: ackernym@gmail.com

I've always been enamored with the magical and fantastic, and that is usually what I try to illustrate. Illustration, and creation in general, is an exercise in figuring out how I can take the magic that is in my mind and share it with others.

Perhaps one of the best ways I've found to create magical elements is to make and work on video games. I'm always thinking of ways on how to turn everyday mundane situations into games that I can share with other people.

In my free time, you can probably find me doing one of several random hobbies that I've picked up over the years such as woodworking, inkmaking. I also will try nearly any food you put in front of me!

For any inquires don't hesistate to reach out!
