EL FIERRO

Release Date: June 24th, 2023

El Fierro is a pixel-platformer game that takes inspiration from the likes of Celeste and is the first full game that I worked on that has been released

My role working on El Fierro was lead artist. I was responsible for the art direction and nearly all the art assets that are seen in-game (save for one fx).

El Fierro is available to play in-browser on itch.io for free!
