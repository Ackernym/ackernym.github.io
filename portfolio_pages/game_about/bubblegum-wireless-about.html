BUBBLEGUM WIRELESS

Release Date: August 14, 2021

Bubblegum Wireless is an endless shooter that I created with a friend for a game jam hosted by the Opera browser that took place in 2021.

I created all the art assets and animations that are seen in the game.

Bubblegum Wireless is available to play in-browser on GameJolt!
